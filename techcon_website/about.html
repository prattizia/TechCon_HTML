Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a local gathering of passionate developers and engineers seeking to exchange ideas and explore innovations. Over the years, it has grown into a global conference attracting thousands of attendees from across the tech industry.

Our Mission

TechCon 2024 is driven by a vision to foster innovation, collaboration, and transformative thinking. We aim to empower individuals and organizations through access to cutting-edge knowledge, breakthrough technologies, and inspiring leadership.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Angela Turing

A pioneer in artificial intelligence, Dr. Turing has contributed to major advances in machine learning and ethical AI development. Her keynote on "Responsible AI" in 2021 set the tone for industry-wide change.

Elijah Kwan

Elijah led the transformation of major cloud infrastructures across Asia. His 2019 session on scalable systems became one of TechCon's most viewed online talks.

Prof. Naomi Alade

An internationally recognized authority in cybersecurity, Prof. Alade emphasized the human side of security and how to build resilient digital communities.
